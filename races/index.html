A variety of race information

Races that I plan on never doing

  • Barkley is a 100+ mile ultra-marathon through the woods of the Cumberland Range in East TN
  • Race Across America (RAAM) is a 3000+ mile cycling race from Santa Monica Pier in Los Angeles to the Empire State Building in New York City
  • Arizona Trail 300 has the catchy tagline of "no entry fee, no prizes, absolutely no support"

Races that I have done

  • Covenant Health Knoxville Marathon
  • Flying Pig Marathon in Cincinnati with a small part across the river in Kentucky
  • Bourbon Chase follows the Kentucky Bourbon trail from Loisville to Lexyington
  • Tennessee Ragnar starts in Chattanooga and ends in Nashville
  • Atlanta Ragnar is a trail run in the Olympic Horse Park (2017)
  • Kentuckiana Ragnar is a trail run (2018)
  • Blue Ridge Relay is a relay run on the Blue Ridge Parkway with the snappy tagline of "Consider all others... a warm-up!" (2017)
